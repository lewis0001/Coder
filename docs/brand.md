# Orbit Brand Guide

## Brand Identity
- **Name:** Orbit
- **Tone & Voice:** Confident, clear, and helpful. Copy is concise with actionable verbs and avoids slang. Use friendly guidance for complex flows (payments, courier handoffs) without being overly casual.
- **Positioning:** A modern super-app for food, shopping, deliveries, and payments with a focus on reliability and transparency.

## Visual Language
- **Primary Color:** `#5B6CFB` (Orbit Indigo)
- **Secondary Color:** `#FF7D64` (Sunset Coral)
- **Neutral Palette:**
  - `#0F172A` (Ink)
  - `#1F2937` (Slate)
  - `#4B5563` (Ash)
  - `#9CA3AF` (Fog)
  - `#E5E7EB` (Mist)
  - `#F8FAFC` (Cloud)
- **Semantic Colors:**
  - Success: `#22C55E`
  - Warning: `#F59E0B`
  - Danger: `#EF4444`
  - Info: `#38BDF8`

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** "Inter", system fallback.
- **Scale:**
  - Display: 32 / 40 / 48
  - Heading: 24 / 28
  - Title: 20
  - Body: 16
  - Caption: 14
- **Weights:** 400 (regular), 600 (semibold), 700 (bold).

## Spacing & Radius
- **Spacing Scale:** 4, 8, 12, 16, 20, 24, 32, 40, 48.
- **Radii:**
  - XS: 6
  - SM: 10
  - MD: 14
  - LG: 20
  - Pill: 999

## Iconography
- Use open-source icon sets such as Phosphor Icons or Heroicons for consistency. Keep line-based icons at 24px with a 2px stroke for clarity. Avoid any proprietary or branded iconography.

## Components
- **Card:** Soft shadow, 14px radius, 1px border in `#E5E7EB`, padding 16–20 depending on context. Cards should be responsive and support list/grid layouts.
- **Button:** Rounded (MD radius), 48px min height, bold label. Primary uses Orbit Indigo background with Cloud text; secondary uses Ink text on Cloud background with 1px border Ink at 10% opacity.
- **Tab Bar (Mobile):** 56–60px height, pill indicator in Orbit Indigo, labels 12–14px. Support RTL mirroring and high-contrast focus outlines.
- **Chips/Pills:** Light backgrounds from secondary/neutral palette, 12px padding, pill radius, clear affordance when selected.

## Accessibility & Localization
- Minimum contrast ratio 4.5:1 for text. Provide focus rings using `#38BDF8` for clarity. Ensure all layout components mirror in RTL and that typography supports Arabic glyphs.
